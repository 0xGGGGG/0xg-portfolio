## G /üngör · analog

Learned to program and English as a kid in 90s Turkey, by cracking games you
couldn't even buy. Stumbled upon demoscene warez groups as the first seeds of the
poison.

Followed that with Bachelors in C.S. and in most of my profession I made real-time
apps (chat, chatbots, game servers, collaborative editors, IoT projects etc.) and
AI systems. Always with deep interest in systems thinking and HCI (Human-Computer
Interaction).

Just before initial Corona Lockdowns (2020 Feb) I came to Berlin, and during first
two years I found myself deeply yearning for physical connection, then getting
genuinely interested in movement and spatial computing too.

I'm building towards one day that I participate and co-own a communal and spatial
computer / brain of a physical space and I invite everyone to the playground.

My inner child wants to play, to observe nature, to build systems, to make
visuals, robots and custom devices. And finally to sharing it while experiencing
it.
