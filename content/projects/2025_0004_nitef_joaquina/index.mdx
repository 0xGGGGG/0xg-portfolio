*Nostalgia is the Extended Feedback* is a solo exhibition by **Joaquina Salgado**
at **SOMA Art, Berlin** — a dim brick hall of inflatable forms, glowing pedestals
and a small screen showing aquatic feedback imagery you steer by hand.

My support was the interactive layer: a **trackball and small screen**, aided with
**electronics and programming**, so visitors could move through the feedback
world of the piece.
