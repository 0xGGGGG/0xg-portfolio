_Nostalgia is the Extended Feedback_ is a game and solo exhibition by **Joaquina Salgado**
at **SOMA Art, Berlin** (2025).

I did **electronics, programming, setup** — a **Raspberry Pi Zero 2**
driving the screens that play the animation loops, and a **Raspberry Pi Pico**
for the trackball.
