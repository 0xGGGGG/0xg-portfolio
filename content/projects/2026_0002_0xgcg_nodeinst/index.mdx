**0xGCG — Grow. Corrupt. Glitch.** is a three-minute immersive video loop for the
projection hall at **Kunstkraftwerk, Leipzig**, developed through the **Node
Institute** *Creative Masterclass: Immersive Room*.

It is a helical meta-narrative of systems — how they emerge, **grow**, **optimize**,
**overfit**, and finally **glitch** into the seed of another dimension, then loop.
Amid the AI boom and its daily dread-and-excitement, I'm co-creating it with my
digital twin, **0xG** (a Claude Code instance), as an open discussion that goes
meta and questions the question itself — not from a protagonist's view but as the
whole system's.

The concept began as a Three.js explorer, deployed separately at
**[0xgcg.0xg.gg](https://0xgcg.0xg.gg)** — scan or follow the link to fly through it.
