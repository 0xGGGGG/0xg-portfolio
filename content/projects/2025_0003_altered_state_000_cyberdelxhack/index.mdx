For the **Cyberdelic Hackathon** (Berlin, 8–9 Nov 2025), with the **Cyberdelic
Nexus Society**, I built a **Meta Quest** mixed-reality app that alters your
vision from real-time sensor data.

Here the signal came from a **microphone** (audio-reactive) and from **touching
fruits and vegetables** (a biotron sensor) — but the idea generalizes to any
sensor: EEG, pressure, heart rate. I later experimented with touching walls and
surfaces to open custom **portals** rendering GLSL shaders I was curating.
