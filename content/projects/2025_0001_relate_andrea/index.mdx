_Relate — Talk With Me_ is an installation by **Andrea Familari** at **uzwei**
in the Dortmunder U, Dortmund — a room where visitors speak with the work and
the work answers.

Developed integration with NLX.ai service between UnrealEngine. Delivered as Blueprint component.
