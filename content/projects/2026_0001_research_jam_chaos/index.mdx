I'm an active member of Chaos Emblematic e.V. joining jams regularly
to perform visuals and bring custom-made tools, devices and scenes
to experiment with — and, more recently, to research.

I'm drawn to **spatial computing** and the live feedback loop between
**movement ⟷ sound ⟷ visuals**, at the same time working across disciplines with others in the
collective.
