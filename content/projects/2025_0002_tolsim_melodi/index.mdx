**TOLSim** is a small physics + mixed-reality builder prototype, made to
experiment with a custom **kinetic joint system**.
There is one type of joint and one type of stick; from
those two primitives you assemble different geometric forms in passthrough MR.

I built it in **Unreal Engine** as an XR app — joints and sticks that snap
together with simulated physics.
